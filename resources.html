Design Thinking and User Experience Design (UX)

  • Human-centered Design resources by leading design firm IDEO.
  • "Design Thinking" by Tim Brown, Harvard Business Review, June 2008. An overview on how thinking like a designer can transform the way you develop products, services, processes – and even strategy.
  • "A Crash Course in Design Thinking from Stanford’s Design School"
  • "UX for Lean Startups. Faster, Smarter User Experience Research and Design" by Laura Klein, O'Reilly, May 2013 (available through U. of Cyprus Digital Library).
